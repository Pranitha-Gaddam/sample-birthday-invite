A cute and simple birthday invite page that is easily customizable with your own name, date, address, and RSVP link.

### How to customize
  <li>Update name, age, date, and time in the header section.</li>
  <li>Replace the address lines with your party location.</li>
  <li>Add your Google Maps link where it says YOUR_LOCATION_LINK_HERE</li>
  <li>If you want RSVP emails, create a Formspree form and replace YOUR_UNIQUE_ID_HERE with your form URL.</li>
